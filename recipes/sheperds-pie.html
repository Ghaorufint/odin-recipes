Sheperd's Pie

Description

Shepherd's pie is a dish consisting of cooked minced meat topped with mashed potato. Its defining ingredients are red meat and onions in gravy or sauce, with a topping of mashed potato. Common variants include adding vegetables to the filling and topping the dish with cheese.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes, coarsely chopped
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped fresh cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 pound beef stew meat, cut into small pieces
  • 3 strips bacon, chopped
  • 3/4 chopped carrot
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Shepherd's pie, cottage pie, or hachis Parmentier is a dish consisting of cooked minced meat topped with mashed potato.[2][3][4] Its defining ingredients are red meat and onions in gravy or sauce, with a topping of mashed potato. Common variants include adding vegetables to the filling and topping the dish with cheese.
  2. Crumble the beef bouillon cube into the water; whisk until the bouillon is dissolved. Pour into a slow cooker. Add the stew meat, bacon, carrot, onion, garlic, and tomato to the slow cooker. Spread the mashed cauliflower and potato mixture over the meat and vegetable mixture. Sprinkle the Cheddar cheese over the top.
  3. Set the slow cooker to Low and cook for 8 hours, or, if you prefer, at High for 4 hours.
